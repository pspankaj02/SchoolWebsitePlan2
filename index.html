About Our School

Established with a vision to provide quality education, C. S. National School has been a beacon of knowledge and values for over two decades. We are committed to holistic development.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and intellectually challenging environment that empowers students to become innovative thinkers, creative problem solvers, and inspired learners.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that fosters a culture of excellence, compassion, and lifelong learning in every student we serve.

Our Values

Integrity, respect, responsibility, and a passion for learning are the core values that guide our entire school community.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet the needs of all learners, from early years to secondary school.

Primary School

Middle School

Co-curricular

Encouraging participation in sports, arts, music, and various clubs for all-round development.
